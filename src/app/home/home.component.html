Welcome to StakSwipe

  • What is StakSwipe

    Stakswipe is a media aggregation app for android with an ios launch soon to come. It uses a card swiping interface to make browsing conent as easy as possible while customizing the content to your liking. Just right swipe the content that you like and left swipe the content you don't like. The app will then customize the cards to your interests.

  • Why use Stakswipe

    Stakswipe allows for the best combination of privacy and automatic customization. With stakswipe all of your interest data is stored on your device. Unlike other social media sites that harvest and sell your interest data as a cost to use their service. With stakswipe you own your data, so you don't have to worry about data breaches or business giving you creepy ads based on that one thing you liked last week.
